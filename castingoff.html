Casting Off

Once you have your gear, it's time to head off!

Make sure you bring plenty of water, some snacks, and a variety of lures and poles!

It's important to remember not to get frustrated; fishing takes practice, and sometimes the fish aren't very active. At changing tides the fish will be more active than still tide, so keep that in mind. Bring a friend along and enjoy the wonderful world of fishing together!

Recommended Gear to Bring:

Item Count
Fishing Poles 4
Shad 8
Poppers 4
Worms 12
Water Bottles 4 per person
Snacks 2 per person
