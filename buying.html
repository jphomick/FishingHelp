Buying Gear

So you're ready to fish you say? Now reel yourself in for a sec.
Do you have the right tools for the job?

You probably think you need a fishing pole to fish. Well, yes and no. I've seen people catch fish with their bare hands, but let's not go in that.
You need a fishing pole.
You also need lures. That's where I come in.

Shad

The shad is your basic lure. It's a crankbait. You throw it in the water, reel it back in, and keep that up until you catch a fish.
Some people get by with just these, but if you only fishing with one of these, you're missing out on half the fun.

Popper

This is a popper. Like a crankbait, you drop it into the water and reel it back...with one exciting difference.
You POP it! Who would have guessed? Simply pull the lure towards your boat and the lure with wriggle with the excitement that fish just gravitate towards.

Worm

When you're ready for a change of pace, bring out the worm. This plastic power-play you drop to the bottom of the waters and brush up against the organic matter floating around down there. You want to brush past a fish that's just hanging out.
